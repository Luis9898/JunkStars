Why choose JunkStars?

JunkStars will save you time and leave you with the satisfaction of having a clean and clutter free space

Leave it to JunkStars to do all the hard work, complicated logistics, and countless hours of fine details that come with junk removal. Our representatives do all the heavy lifting for you and will restore access to forgotten space that was taken up by clutter. We provide you with the peace of mind that comes with having a safe and hazard free environment. Don’t forget JunkStars will reduce your environmental footprint by recycling and reclaiming items from jobsites. JunkStars will also proudly donate back to the community any items deemed acceptable to be reused. Thank you for choosing JunkStars.

About Us

We are a local Texas family-owned business, with deep rooted traditional values in our personal and professional lives. We started our journey as a start-up founded by two brothers from scratch with little to no resources or capital. However, with the help of family members and great friends JunkStars has grown into an efficient, disciplined, and professional company ready to meet all of your hauling needs. We will continue to serve our community and strive to deliver top quality hauling solutions that are in line with our mission of providing superior quality service to all customers.
