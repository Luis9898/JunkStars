About Us

We are a local Texas family-owned business, with deep rooted traditional values in our personal and professional lives. We started our journey as a start-up founded by two brothers from scratch with little to no resources or capital. However, with the help of family members and great friends JunkStars has grown into an efficient, disciplined, and professional company ready to meet all of your hauling needs. We will continue to serve our community and strive to deliver top quality hauling solutions thats in line with our mission of providing superior quality service to all customers.
